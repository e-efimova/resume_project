The personality of Zero Two is dangerous, but attractive. She is an elite pilot with the pseudonym "Partner Killer" ( パートナー殺し, Pātonā-Goroshi).

A hybrid of a human and a blob, which is a member of the APE special group. He knows a lot about the world in which people lived on the surface. But her knowledge of the past world of people is not all that she can surprise — Zero Two understands feelings that are alien to the members of the thirteenth squad. The true nature of her emotions and character is hidden by the rim, which is attached to the girl's horns..

Teaming up with Hiro and awakening the true power of "Strelitzia", saves Plantation-13 from destruction.After reuniting with the thirteenth squad, the character of Zero Two has undergone changes: the girl has become much kinder and more respectful to others, and also began to follow the rules. After the last battle in space , she was reborn on Earth, together with Hiro in the body of an ordinary person.
